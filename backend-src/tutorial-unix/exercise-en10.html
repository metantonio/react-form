10 Directory Deletion

We can delete folders with the rm folder_name command, a short form of the word "remove".

It is common for folders to contain files and subdirectories. In such cases, a recursive deletion is necessary with rm -r folder_name, where folder_name is the name of the folder.

It is also possible, and indeed quite useful, to specify where the folder will be deleted without having to move from our current directory, using relative paths.
